Serbian Cultural Association

Pontes-Mostovi

Trieste, Italy

Since 2008 we preserve and value the culture, tradition and immaterial cultural heritage of Serbia.

The Serbian Cultural Association "Pontes-Mostovi" was founded with the desire of preserve and transmit the musical and folklore heritage of Serbia. Through folk dances, songs and traditional clothing, we tell a story about a nation rich in colors, melodies and traditions spanning across centuries.


More than 100 people are today part of our big family. Divided in 5 groups according to age, they learn traditional Serbian dances and give them new life by performing in front of the local and international public. Our Association had the great honour to take part in many events, festivals and concerts in Italy and in Europe, performing also in Spain, France, Austria, Hungary, Czech Republic and many more.
